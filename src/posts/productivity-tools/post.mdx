Every time I format my computer I literally forget all the nice tools that I had installed in my computer. So here I will keep a track of all the tools that I have used.

# david

Shortcut to open urls with params

# copyclip

Create history of all your copies

# 1password

Saves all your passwords

# evernote

screenshot and take notes

# skitch

Put nice small markings in your screenshot
